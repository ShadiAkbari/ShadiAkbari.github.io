About Me

I’m a student on this little blue planet (hello, aliens if you’re out there!) fascinated by the universe. My research focuses on cosmology, especially the early universe, but I’m constantly exploring a wide range of topics—from cosmic inflation and non-Gaussianity to simulations, machine learning, and symbolic calculations in General Relativity. Basically, I’m trying to learn a bit about everything!
